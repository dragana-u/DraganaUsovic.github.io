Introducing me

My name is Dragana, and I'm from Ohrid. I recently completed my first year of education at the Faculty of Computer and Science Engineeringin Skopje. My primary areas of study are software engineering and information systems, and I have so far mastered C and C++. In addition, I'm proficient in HTML and CSS, and my next objective is to master JavaScript.

In addition to expanding my knowledge in the field I am studying, my time at FINKI has also allowed me to meet new people and form friendships that will last a lifetime. Collaborating with others in a group setting and sharing knowledge and experience has improved both my interpersonal and leadership skills. I have always thought of myself as a leader, not afraid to break the ice and help my team succeed.

As Fyodor Dostoyevsky once said, The mystery of human existence lies not in just staying alive but in finding something to live for. I live for establishing a career as a software engineer and building connections to never have a dull day in my life.
